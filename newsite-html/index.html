:~$Bio

My life happened immersed in extreme experiences. Grow up travelling, spent my youth splitting my time between the nature at the heart of Cerrado and computers in Brasilia, capital of Brazil.

That's why (when I have time) I like to dance, enjoy nature by practicing radical sports, or I meditate on silence about human relationship with our environment.

(pause) writing a bio is uncomfortable. Let's make it short and painless.

My father, who is a civil engineer, thaught me how to program when I was 12. He teached me also that subversion and liberty are inside heart, even if you dress military clothes.

I **strongly** believe that technology exists for change. We "live" in a world that needs to be more equal and responsible - tech isn't a miracle, it's a system.


We have to take control of this system.
